What Is Follicular Unit Extraction (FUE)

Minimally invasive hair restoration with no linear scarring

Follicular Unit Extraction (FUE) is a surgical procedure that improves hair regeneration through a minimally invasive hair transplant. Individual follicle units are extracted from the donor area and placed in the recipient area for permanent hair regeneration.

Unlike FUT, FUE harvests individual hair follicles rather than removing a hair strip from the donor area, resulting in minimal to no surgical scar and hair restoration that appears seamless and natural.

FUE, like FUT, has an amazing and completely natural appearance. Both procedures ensure that the hair transplant result is natural and permanent.

The FUE Procedure

Two key stages of Follicular Unit Extraction

Donor Area Preparation

The first step is to identify the donor area from which healthy and strong follicles will be extracted. Hair trimming occurs in the identified donor area to facilitate the extraction of individual healthy grafts.

Follicle Extraction

After identifying healthy follicles, these grafts are separated from the rest of the hair and extracted very gently. Tiny incisions are made to isolate and extract individual grafts with tiny tweezer-like instruments. This procedure is performed under a microscope to ensure that only healthy grafts are extracted precisely and safely.

Recovery & Results

The incision marks are barely visible and will heal within a few days because this is a minimally invasive procedure. Hair regeneration is visible after 3 to 6 months and the result is permanent.

FAQ for International Patients

Common questions about FUE hair transplant for our international patients

Is FUE better than FUT?

Both FUE and FUT are excellent procedures, and the best choice depends on your individual situation:

  • FUE Benefits - No linear scar, quicker recovery, easier to wear short hairstyles
  • FUT Benefits - Can harvest more grafts in one session, often more cost-effective per graft

At Barley, we combine both methods with our microneedle implantation technology for optimal results. During your consultation, we'll assess your hair characteristics and goals to recommend the best approach for you.

Will there be visible scarring with FUE?

FUE results in minimal, barely visible scarring:

  • Tiny punch scars - Small, dot-like scars rather than a linear scar
  • Heals quickly - Scabs form and fall off within days
  • Hair coverage - Donor area hair grows back and covers the tiny scars
  • Short hairstyles OK - Most patients can wear their hair very short without visible scarring

With Barley's advanced extraction techniques, the scars are typically so small that they're barely noticeable, even with close inspection.

How long is the FUE recovery period?

FUE recovery is relatively quick and comfortable:

  • First 2-3 days - Mild discomfort, scabs form, can return to desk work
  • 3-7 days - Scabs fall off, swelling subsides, more normal activities
  • 1-2 weeks - Back to most normal activities, including light exercise
  • 2-4 weeks - Full recovery, can resume all normal activities including vigorous exercise
  • 3-6 months - New hair begins growing visibly

With Barley's microneedle technology, recovery is even faster - typically 2-3 days for the implantation area to heal!

How many FUE sessions will I need?

The number of sessions depends on your individual goals and situation:

  • Single session - Many patients achieve their goals with one well-planned session
  • 1,500-3,000 grafts - Typical for one FUE session
  • Multiple sessions - Sometimes recommended for extensive hair loss or to build density over time
  • Healing time - 6-12 months between sessions if needed

During your consultation, we'll assess your donor hair availability and restoration goals to create a personalized treatment plan that may involve one or multiple sessions.

Can I have FUE if I've had previous hair transplants?

Yes! FUE is often an excellent choice for transplant repair or enhancement:

  • Repair procedures - Can fix unsatisfactory results from previous transplants
  • Adding density - FUE can fill in sparse areas from earlier procedures
  • Donor area conservation - FUE can harvest from areas not used in previous FUT procedures
  • Natural refinement - Can refine and enhance hairlines or density

Our doctors have extensive experience in repair procedures and can assess whether FUE is suitable for your specific situation. Bring photos of your previous procedure if possible!
