Our Milestones

Growth journey and important milestones of Barley Microneedle Hair Transplant

2006
Dr. Li Xingdong introduced microneedle hair transplant technology to China.
2010
Barley sent an initiative letter to the Ministry of Health of the People's Republic of China, calling for May 28 to be designated as National Hair Care Day.
2015
Barley Hair Transplant Medical Group Co., Ltd. was established.
2015
Barley became the Clinical Research Base for Hair Transplantation of the Medical Aesthetics Professional Committee of the China Association of Chinese and Western Medicine.
2016
Became China's first medical group to offer microneedle hair transplant, and co-organized China's first International Hair Transplant Academic Conference.
2018
Published the "2018 Microneedle Hair Transplant Technology Standard White Paper" and was invited to attend the 26th World Hair Transplant Conference.
2019
Brand upgrade and launched no-shave hair transplant technology.
2021
Dr. Li Xingdong was invited to attend the 5th China Hair Transplant Conference and served as the main drafting unit of the "Group Standard for Hair Transplant Specifications."
2024
Barley Microneedle Hair Transplant and global consulting firm Sullivan jointly released the "New Microneedle Hair Transplant Standard Setting White Paper."
2025
Barley Microneedle Hair Transplant was invited to participate in the Hong Kong Asia Pacific Medical Conference (AMSC Asia) and delivered a keynote speech, receiving recognition from international peers.
