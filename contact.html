Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about contacting us and getting started

How quickly will I get a response to my inquiry?

We aim to respond to all inquiries as quickly as possible:

  • WhatsApp/WeChat - Often within minutes during business hours
  • Email - Usually within 24 hours
  • Business hours - Monday to Sunday, China Standard Time
  • Time zone difference - We understand and work around time differences

We have team members monitoring communications at various hours to serve our international patients well!

What information should I include in my first message?

To help us assist you best, please include:

  • Photos - Clear photos of your current hair situation
  • Your goals - What you hope to achieve
  • Timeline - When you're considering the procedure
  • Location - Which country/city you're coming from
  • Questions - Any specific concerns you have

The more information you provide upfront, the better and faster we can help you!

Can I get a preliminary assessment through photos?

Yes, we can provide preliminary assessments:

  • Photo evaluation - Our doctors can assess your situation remotely
  • Treatment recommendations - Get suggested approaches
  • Cost estimate - Receive a preliminary quote
  • Timeframe - Understand how long you'll need to stay
  • No obligation - Assessment is free and no pressure

While preliminary assessments are helpful, an in-person consultation is always best for final planning!

Do you provide assistance with travel arrangements?

Yes, we provide comprehensive support for international patients:

  • Visa letters - Official invitation letters for visa applications
  • Hotel recommendations - Partner hotels at special rates
  • Airport pickup - Available upon request
  • Local transportation - Help getting around
  • City tours - Recommendations if you want to explore

We want your entire experience - from travel to treatment - to be as smooth as possible!

Is there any cost or obligation for the initial consultation?

No, our initial consultation is completely free:

  • No consultation fee - Assessment is complimentary
  • No obligation - No pressure to proceed
  • All questions welcome - Ask anything you want
  • Honest assessment - We'll tell you what we realistically can achieve
  • Take your time - Decide at your own pace

We believe in providing helpful information and letting you make the decision that's best for you!

What languages do you support for communication?

We support multiple languages for international patients:

  • English - Full support for English-speaking patients
  • Translation services - Professional translators available
  • Chinese - Our native language for local patients
  • WhatsApp/WeChat - Easy text communication with translation
  • Video calls - Available with interpreters if needed

We're committed to making sure communication is never a barrier to getting the care you want!

How far in advance should I book my procedure?

Booking timeline recommendations:

  • International patients - 4-8 weeks in advance recommended
  • Visa processing - Allow time for visa application
  • Travel arrangements - Better prices and options with early booking
  • Doctor availability - Ensure your preferred doctor is available
  • Flexibility - Last minute bookings sometimes possible

Contact us as soon as you're thinking about it - we can help you plan the timing perfectly!

Can I combine my procedure with tourism in China?

Absolutely! Many patients combine treatment with tourism:

  • Recovery time - Plan to stay extra days to explore
  • Major cities - Shanghai, Beijing, and others have so much to offer
  • Cultural experience - Rich history, cuisine, and attractions
  • Shopping - World-class shopping opportunities
  • Recommendations - We're happy to suggest places to visit

Many patients tell us their trip became a wonderful medical and cultural experience!
