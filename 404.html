Our Revolutionary Technology

Microneedle implant pen vs traditional hair transplant

Barley Microneedle Implant Pen

Microneedle hair transplant, also known as implant pen technology, works by placing individual hair follicles into the hollow tip of the implant pen, then directly pushing the follicle into the pre-designed recipient area of the scalp. The pen can be rotated 360° to match natural hair growth direction, significantly reducing trauma and patient pain while avoiding secondary follicle damage.

  • Smaller incision (0.6-1.0mm) - 1/3 smaller
  • Minimal trauma, fast recovery
  • Wash hair in 24 hours
  • 20-30% higher density
  • 360° rotation for natural direction

How soon will I see results from my hair transplant?

Hair transplant results progress through several stages:

  • Month 12 - Full, mature results with complete thickness
